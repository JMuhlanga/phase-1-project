About Us

Spice E-Sports was formed in 2022 by Dev-Knights & CO.The current gaming community in Kenya is more or less social and people with talent and can hack it in the E-sports scene are not getting the necessary recognition of their talents in gaming . This industry is also untapped for business who want to market their products and people who want to earn from their talents.

We would like to bring together like minded individuals into a community of E-sports Gamers. E-sports can be a means to unite people and become a form of release after a long week of hard work . It can be a viable form of entertainment for people if only we can form various leagues based on the games and accompany them with the relevant rewards.

Spice E-sports aims to creating an avenue to give this recognition to notable people and teams that have achieved at their various forms of e-sports while also getting details of people that want to join the scene and participate in the same.
